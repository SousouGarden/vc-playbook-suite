# Clause Casebook — 30+ 条款的反打实战

> v1.0 首版。与主文件 Phase 2-3 绑定使用。

**本文件的格式**：对每条关键 TS 条款，展示：
1. **VC 的 opening move**（他们 TS 里常写的版本 + 通常附带话术）
2. **创始人的第一反打**（你初次 push back 的话术）
3. **让步阶梯**（从 best case 到 walk-away 的 5 级让步）
4. **trade-off 选项**（这条让步可以换什么）

每条按 Economics / Control / Founder / Mechanics 分类。

---

## Economics 类

---

### 条款 1：Pre-Money Valuation

**VC opening**：「Pre-money $25M」（常低于你想要的）

**VC 话术**：
- 「Market conditions are tough」
- 「Your metrics are behind comparable companies」
- 「We're taking on early-stage risk」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Let me push back with specifics. [Comparable A] raised Series A at
$35M pre three months ago with [similar metric]. [Comparable B] at $45M
pre last quarter. I think $32-38M is the market range. Where did you
get $25M?"
```

**让步阶梯**（假设你目标 $35M）：
- Level 1（best case）：$38M pre + clean terms
- Level 2：$35M pre + clean terms
- Level 3：$32M pre + clean terms + bigger check size
- Level 4：$30M pre + clean terms + 1 board seat to founder-friendly angel
- Level 5（walk-away）：$28M pre

**Trade 选项**：
- 接受 $30M pre → 换取 1x Non-Participating + post-money option pool
- 接受 $28M pre → 换取 $2M larger check + founder control preservation

---

### 条款 2：Amount Raised

**VC opening**：「We lead with $10M on $25M pre」

**VC 话术**：
- 「Optimal check size for our portfolio construction」
- 「Any bigger would be too dilutive」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Given the plan we discussed — [18 month milestone to X], we need $12-15M
to execute with a cushion. A $10M check at $25M pre gets me [runway]
months, which lands me exactly at next milestone with no buffer. Let's
size it right."
```

**让步阶梯**（你目标 $12M）：
- Level 1：$15M at $35M pre
- Level 2：$12M at $32M pre
- Level 3：$10M at $30M pre + co-investor for $3M
- Level 4：$10M at $28M pre
- Level 5（walk）：$8M at $25M pre

**Trade 选项**：
- Smaller check from lead → 换取你 reserve capacity for co-investors you trust

---

### 条款 3：Option Pool Size

**VC opening**：「20% post-closing option pool, out of pre-money」

**VC 话术**：
- 「You need this for hiring」
- 「Market standard is 15-20% pool」
- 「Option pool always comes out of pre-money」

**你的第一反打（关键 must-win）**：
```
"Two pushes on this one.

First, let's walk through the hiring plan. Over the next 18 months, we
plan to hire [N people]. If I model comp for each at target grants,
that's ~[%]% of the pool needed — let's say [X]%. Happy to show you
the spreadsheet. So a [X]% pool is right-sized. 20% means I'm paying
for hires I don't need.

Second, on pre vs post — let's do the math. If [X]% out of pre-money,
my effective pre is [Y, which is less]. If out of post-money, it's
shared proportionally. I think post-money is more aligned with us being
partners through the round."
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1（best case）：10% out of post-money
- Level 2：12.5% out of post-money
- Level 3：15% out of post-money
- Level 4：15% half pre / half post
- Level 5（walk-able）：15% out of pre-money

**Trade 选项**：
- 接受 pre-money pool → 换取 valuation bump (+15-20%)
- 接受 post-money pool but 20% → 换取 faster closing

---

### 条款 4：Liquidation Preference — Type

**VC opening**：「1x Participating Preferred」（Participating 是红旗版本）

**VC 话术**：
- 「Standard protection for our downside」
- 「We've done participating for the last 5 deals」
- 「This is market in this round size」

**你的第一反打（绝对 must-win）**：
```
"Participating is not 2025 market for a $30M+ pre-money Series A.
Non-Participating is. Here's my evidence:

- NVCA published data: 78% of Series A are Non-Participating
- [3 specific recent rounds] all Non-Participating
- My lawyer confirms this is the drift

On top of that, Participating creates misaligned incentives at exit.
If we get an $80M offer that returns 4x, Participating creates a weird
negotiation where you'd rather sell at $60M. I don't want that future
conversation.

Let's do 1x Non-Participating."
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1（must-win）：1x Non-Participating
- Level 2：1x Capped Participating at 2x cap
- Level 3：1x Capped Participating at 3x cap（ugly）
- Level 4（almost walk）：1x Participating
- Level 5（walk）：2x Participating 或 2x Non-Participating

**Trade 选项**：
- 接受 1x Capped Participating at 3x → 换取 +10% valuation
- 接受 1x Non-Participating (standard) → 给 VC some other ask (pro-rata / board obs)

---

### 条款 5：Liquidation Preference — Multiple

**VC opening**：「2x Non-Participating」

**VC 话术**：
- 「Risk-adjusted return expectation」
- 「Hot market / defensive financing」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"2x Non-Participating is not standard. It's bridge / distressed round
territory. This is neither. Let's be at 1x.

If the concern is downside, we can structure ratchets: if we don't
hit [milestone X] by [date Y], Liquidation Preference bumps to 1.5x.
But starting at 2x is not Series A 2026."
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1（must-win）：1x
- Level 2：1.5x
- Level 3（walk-level）：2x

---

### 条款 6：Anti-Dilution

**VC opening**：「Full Ratchet anti-dilution」（极端 opening）

**VC 话术**：
- 「Protection against down rounds」
- 「Standard in our firm」

**你的第一反打（red line）**：
```
"Full ratchet is not standard. It's distressed. Standard is Broad-Based
Weighted Average. Here's why:

Under FR, if we raise a down round at 80% of current, you get fully
repriced. Founder takes all the dilution. This creates total misalignment
— you have no incentive to support my second round if economy shifts.

Broad-based WA aligns us. Everyone shares the pain proportionally."
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1（must-win）：Broad-Based Weighted Average
- Level 2：Narrow-Based Weighted Average
- Level 3（walk）：Full Ratchet

**Trade 选项**：
- 接受 Pay-to-Play clause (investor 不 participate 下轮就失去 anti-dilution) 作为反制
- 接受 cap on anti-dilution（e.g., only if下轮 < 70% of current）

---

### 条款 7：Dividend

**VC opening**：「Cumulative dividend 8%」

**VC 话术**：
- 「Time value of money protection」
- 「Optional when exit happens, just protects against delay」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Cumulative dividend creates accrued obligation that effectively bumps
your LP to 1.5-2x over 5 years, without any underlying change in value
creation. Let's either:
(a) No dividend, or
(b) Non-cumulative (only if declared)"
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1：No dividend
- Level 2：Non-cumulative dividend
- Level 3：Cumulative 6% (not 8%)
- Level 4（walk）：Cumulative 8%+

## Control 类

---

### 条款 8：Board Composition

**VC opening**：「3-person board: 1 Founder + 1 Investor + 1 jointly elected independent」

**VC 话术**：
- 「Standard for Series A」
- 「Need oversight for $12M check」

**你的第一反打（must-win）**：
```
"At Series A, I want the balance that gives me room to operate while
having useful input.

Proposed: 5-person board. 2 Founders (myself + [co-founder]), 1 you,
2 independent — chosen by consensus.

Reasoning: odd number prevents deadlock. 2 founders reflects that this
is a founder-led company. 1 investor seat is sufficient for your
oversight. 2 independents bring expertise (we'd pick someone like
[name of potential independent])."
```

**让步阶梯**（3 founders want vs VC 想 majority）：
- Level 1：2F + 1 VC + 2 Independent
- Level 2：2F + 1 VC + 1 Independent (4 seats, risk of tie)
- Level 3：1F + 1 VC + 1 Independent (3 seats)
- Level 4：1F + 2 VC + 1 Independent (bad for founder)
- Level 5（walk-worthy）：1F + 2 VC + 1 Independent + VC veto

**Trade 选项**：
- 接受 VC 有 board seat → 换取 founder 选择 independent 的 unilateral right
- 接受 fewer board seats → 换取 board observer rights for other strategic angels

---

### 条款 9：Protective Provisions (Preferred Veto)

**VC opening**：「Preferred majority required for: sale, charter changes, new preferred, dividend, debt > $500K, business change, CEO hire/fire, option pool changes, subsidiary creation, IP licensing outside ordinary course...」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Let me separate these into 3 tiers:

Tier A (protect LP interest — agree):
- Sale, charter, new preferred, dividend

Tier B (company operating flexibility — need higher threshold or remove):
- Debt > $500K → raise to $2M (company operating)
- Business change → define 'substantial', keep for material changes only
- Subsidiary creation → keep but exempt operational subs

Tier C (founder operating flexibility — remove):
- CEO hire/fire (board has this)
- Option pool changes (board has this)
- IP licensing outside ordinary course → too vague, remove

Let's pare down to Tier A + thoughtful B."
```

**让步阶梯**（focus on keeping Tier C out）：
- Level 1：Only Tier A
- Level 2：Tier A + narrow Tier B
- Level 3：Tier A + all Tier B
- Level 4（walk-level）：Tier A + B + some Tier C

---

### 条款 10：Drag-Along Rights

**VC opening**：「Majority of Preferred + Majority of Common trigger drag-along; 50% / 50% threshold」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Drag-along is important for M&A flexibility. Three asks:

1. Threshold bump to 66% of Preferred + 50% of Common (ensures broad
   consensus)
2. Founder consent during employment (while I'm CEO, sale needs my vote
   too)
3. Minimum price floor: drag can only be exercised if offer ≥ 2x
   current post-money (prevents fire-sale drag)"
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1：66% Preferred + 50% Common + founder consent during employment + 2x floor
- Level 2：66% Preferred + 50% Common + founder consent while employed
- Level 3：50% Preferred + 50% Common + founder consent for 2 years
- Level 4：50% / 50% with no floor, no founder consent (standard but risky)

**Trade 选项**：
- 接受 lower threshold (66% → 50%) → 换取 founder consent right

---

### 条款 11：Information Rights

**VC opening**：「Weekly metrics dashboard + monthly financials + quarterly board meetings + unlimited inspection rights」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Info rights should be meaningful without being operational drag:
- Monthly financials: OK (we do it anyway)
- Quarterly board meetings: OK
- Annual audit: OK
- Weekly dashboard: too much. Let's do bi-weekly dashboard (what we
  send to team)
- Unlimited inspection: replace with 'reasonable inspection with 5 days
  notice'"
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1：Monthly + quarterly + reasonable inspection
- Level 2：Monthly + quarterly + weekly dashboard
- Level 3：Monthly + quarterly + weekly dashboard + unlimited inspection

---

### 条款 12：Right of First Refusal (Preferred)

**VC opening**：「Investor ROFR on any Common sale」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"ROFR is reasonable for material founder sales. But let's carve out:
- ESOP-related sales (employees exiting, secondary sales up to
  $500K/year per person) — no ROFR
- Transfer to family trusts / estate planning — no ROFR
- Small founder liquidity (<20% of founder holding) — no ROFR for
  first $2M to let founder take some chips off"
```

---

### 条款 13：Co-Sale Rights (Common)

**VC opening**：「Investor has co-sale on any founder sale of common」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Same carve-outs as ROFR. And let's say co-sale is limited to material
sales (>$1M transactions). Small secondary transactions for team
retention should be exempt."
```

---

### 条款 14：Pay-to-Play

**VC opening**：「Not included」（VC usually doesn't add this）

**你主动添加**（创始人友好条款）：
```
"I want to add a Pay-to-Play provision: if an investor doesn't
participate pro-rata in next round, their Preferred converts to
Common. This aligns LP behavior — they can't be hands-off in bad
times and still enjoy downside protection."
```

**VC 可能反对**：「不要 — limits 我们 flexibility」

**Trade**：接受你主动引入 pay-to-play → VC accept slightly higher valuation 或 smaller board footprint

## Founder-Specific 类

---

### 条款 15：Founder Vesting — Terms

**VC opening**：「4-year vesting, 1-year cliff, standard」

**你的第一反打（ok on structure, push on credit）**：
```
"Agree on structure. Two asks:

1. Vesting credit for prior work: I've been building this for 18 months.
   I want 18 months of vesting credit at closing. So starting day 1:
   37.5% already vested, balance over 30 months.

2. Acceleration: I want double-trigger acceleration — if company is
   acquired AND I'm terminated or my role is materially changed within
   12 months, 100% of remaining shares accelerate."
```

**让步阶梯（credit）**：
- Level 1：18 months credit + double-trigger 100%
- Level 2：12 months credit + double-trigger 100%
- Level 3：6 months credit + double-trigger 50%
- Level 4：0 credit + double-trigger 50%

**让步阶梯（acceleration）**：
- Level 1：Double-trigger 100% + single-trigger 50%
- Level 2：Double-trigger 100%
- Level 3：Double-trigger 50%
- Level 4：No acceleration

---

### 条款 16：Founder Employment — Termination

**VC opening**：「Founder can be terminated 'for cause' by Board majority」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Agree Board can terminate for cause, but two key points:
1. 'For cause' must be strictly defined — list specific behaviors
   (fraud, material breach, felony), not 'failure to meet
   performance expectations'
2. 'Without cause' termination gets accelerated vesting + severance
   (6 months) + retained title for IP assignments already transferred"
```

**让步阶梯**（tight definition of cause）：
- Level 1：Tight cause + 100% acceleration without cause + 12 months severance
- Level 2：Tight cause + 50% acceleration without cause + 6 months severance
- Level 3：Tight cause + no acceleration + 3 months severance
- Level 4：Loose cause + no acceleration + 0 severance

---

### 条款 17：Founder Non-Compete

**VC opening**：「3-year non-compete post termination, global」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Non-compete needs to be reasonable and enforceable. CA and some other
states don't enforce these. Two asks:

1. Duration: 12 months (not 3 years), with possible extension if VC
   pays during extended period
2. Scope: specific category, not 'global' or 'any competing
   activity'"
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1：12 month specific category
- Level 2：18 month specific category
- Level 3：24 month specific + paid extension option
- Level 4（walk）：3 year global

---

### 条款 18：Founder IP Assignment

**VC opening**：「Founder assigns all past and future IP related to company」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Future IP from company work: agree. But:
- Past IP: only IP created for this company, not general prior
  expertise
- Side projects: explicit carve-out for projects explicitly outside
  company scope
- Open source contributions: clarified as personal, not company IP
  unless contributed through company channels"
```

---

### 条款 19：Change of Control Trigger

**VC opening**：「Change of control limited to company's complete sale/merger」

**你主动添加** — 让创始人 leverage on M&A:
```
"Let's define Change of Control to include:
- Sale of all or substantially all assets
- Merger where Common + Common proxy < 50% of surviving entity
- >50% of Preferred sold to new entity
- Board composition change >50% within 12 months

This triggers acceleration and ensures I'm not squeezed in M&A."
```

## Mechanics 类

---

### 条款 20：No-Shop / Exclusivity

**VC opening**：「60 days exclusive negotiation」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"30 days standard. Let's do 30 with extensions by mutual agreement
based on DD progress. If we're still actively closing on day 30,
extend 15 days."
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1：30 days + 15-day conditional extension
- Level 2：45 days
- Level 3：60 days
- Level 4（walk）：90 days

---

### 条款 21：Expenses / Legal Fees

**VC opening**：「Company covers investor legal fees, up to $100K」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"$100K is above standard for this round size. Standard is $50K for
Series A. I'll agree $50K, or alternatively $75K with a walk-back if
deal falls through."
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1：$40K cap
- Level 2：$50K cap
- Level 3：$75K cap + walk-back if no close
- Level 4：$100K cap + walk-back if no close
- Level 5（walk）：$100K+ no walk-back

---

### 条款 22：Closing Conditions

**VC opening**：「Subject to satisfactory DD, key person agreements, legal opinion, and approval by Investment Committee」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"'Satisfactory DD' is too vague. Let's specify:
- DD completion within 30 days of TS signing
- 'No material adverse change' defined precisely (not subjective)
- Key person agreements attached as schedule to TS
- IC approval confirmed at TS signing (not pending)"
```

**Red flag**：如果 VC 不能 confirm IC approval before TS → likely he's using TS to get you committed while他们内部还没批准。

---

### 条款 23：Post-Closing Conditions

**VC opening**：「Company to complete [X items] within 90 days of closing」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Post-closing items OK as TS-side commitments, but:
- Each item has clear scope + deadline
- Financial penalties for miss are limited (not bank default)
- If missed in good faith, 30-day cure period"
```

---

### 条款 24：Amendment / Waiver

**VC opening**：「Changes require majority of preferred」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Standard. But I want to confirm: if future preferred dilutes me
further, I want veto right on changes to my rights (e.g., if a later
round tries to reduce my acceleration or founder seats, I can block)."
```

## 新型条款 (2024-2026 Era)

---

### 条款 25：Right to Purchase Next Round Amount

**VC opening**：「Pro-rata rights for participating pro-rata in next round」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Standard pro-rata is 1x (VC can buy their proportion again). Two asks:
1. Limit to VCs who have participated in all previous rounds (no
   latecomers with pro-rata on full history)
2. Super pro-rata (1.5x-2x pro-rata) not granted at Series A — this
   is Series B+ topic"
```

---

### 条款 26：ESG / Impact Provisions

**VC opening**：「Company commits to annual ESG reporting + diversity metrics」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Fine with reporting. Two comments:
1. Don't let reporting become performative: focus on metrics that
   matter to us as company
2. No punishment clauses — can't tie reporting to funding release
   or board voting rights"
```

---

### 条款 27：AI Usage Provisions

**VC opening**（新，2026 常见）：「Company commits to not train on competitor data; commits to safety alignment; RLHF audit quarterly」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"These are great aspirations but need careful definition:
- Don't require us to forgo data we legally have
- 'Safety alignment' needs a spec (specific frameworks, not
  aspirational)
- RLHF audit quarterly is overkill; annual + on-demand OK"
```

---

### 条款 28：Exit Preference Stacking

**VC opening**：「This round's preference seniority over Series Seed」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Stacking creates uneven incentive alignment across rounds. Let's do
pari passu with all prior preferred — everyone lines up together."
```

**让步阶梯**：
- Level 1：pari passu with all rounds
- Level 2：pari passu with immediate prior round, senior to prior
- Level 3：senior stacking (worst for founder)

---

### 条款 29：MFN (Most Favored Nation) Clause

**VC opening**：「Investor gets MFN on all terms from subsequent rounds」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"MFN only on economics (preference, anti-dilution) not on governance
terms (board, votes). Governance tailored to each round's needs."
```

---

### 条款 30：Data Room Requirements

**VC opening**：「Full data room with all contracts, customer, finance, employee details」

**你的第一反打**：
```
"Data room by materiality tier:
- Tier 1 (before TS): high-level metrics + redacted contracts
- Tier 2 (TS signed, pre-close): employee list, top customers,
  major contracts
- Tier 3 (post-close): full detail as needed
- Customer data: anonymized before TS, de-anonymized post-close only"
```

## 跨条款的 "Trade Stack" 总览

实战中，谈判不是一条一条谈，是整体交换。以下 5 个 trade stack 最常见：

### Trade Stack A：「Economics for Control」
创始人接受 lower valuation → 换取 stronger board control + no-veto on operating

### Trade Stack B：「Pro-rata for Preference」
接受 VC more pro-rata rights → 换取 1x Non-Participating (instead of Participating)

### Trade Stack C：「Founder Rights for Investor Rights」
接受 VC better info rights + inspection → 换取 tight founder employment terms + 100% acceleration

### Trade Stack D：「Speed for Price」
接受 smaller raise amount + smaller check → 换取 faster closing + minimal DD friction

### Trade Stack E：「Founder Hat Time for Board Hat Time」
接受 some protective provisions → 换取 founder retention rights + compensation protection

## Final checklist — 签 TS 前的 24 小时自检

- [ ] Real valuation (after terms) 我算过了 ≥ headline valuation 的 85%
- [ ] 3 must-win 都 locked
- [ ] 没有 Full Ratchet
- [ ] 没有 2x+ Participating
- [ ] 没有 aggressive milestones tied to funding
- [ ] Board 有足够 independent 且非 VC-chosen
- [ ] Protective provisions 不进运营层
- [ ] Drag-along 有 threshold + founder consent + price floor
- [ ] Founder vesting 有 credit + double-trigger
- [ ] Non-compete 合理
- [ ] No-shop ≤ 45 days
- [ ] Legal fees cap ≤ $75K with walk-back
- [ ] Closing conditions specific
- [ ] Amendment 有我的 veto
